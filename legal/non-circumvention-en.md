# Non-Circumvention & Non-Solicitation Clause — HeyHomie

_Applies to: cleaners ("homies") under contract with **[Company]**, and referenced
in the client Terms of Service._

## 1. Purpose
Clients are introduced to homies exclusively through the HeyHomie platform. This
clause protects the Company's legitimate business interests, its insurance cover,
and service quality, while remaining consistent with the freedom of competition and
labour law.

## 2. Definitions
- **Platform** — the HeyHomie apps and services operated by [Company].
- **Introduced client** — any client whose contact or engagement arose through the
  Platform.
- **Off-platform engagement** — provision of cleaning or related services to an
  Introduced client outside the Platform, with payment made outside the system.

## 3. Non-solicitation / non-circumvention (homie)
During the contract and for **[X] months** after its termination, the homie shall
not, directly or indirectly, solicit, encourage, or accept an Off-platform
engagement with an Introduced client. All bookings, scheduling and payment for such
clients must be arranged through the Platform.

## 4. Contractual penalty
Each breach of Section 3 entitles the Company to a contractual penalty of
**[amount] PLN** (Polish Civil Code, art. 483–484), without prejudice to the right
to claim damages exceeding the penalty on general terms. The penalty applies to
non-monetary obligations and does not limit the homie's statutory rights.

## 5. Detection and monitoring
Because missions are auto-assigned and homies indicate their working availability,
the Company can identify irregular patterns. Any monitoring is limited to what is
necessary and proportionate, is disclosed in advance, and complies with the GDPR /
RODO and applicable labour law. The Company does not monitor private life.

## 6. Insurance, risk and liability
The Company is insured and bears the operational risks **only** for services
performed through the Platform. Services provided by a homie **off-platform or
outside their working hours are not covered** by the Company's insurance or quality
guarantee, and the Company accepts no liability for them; the homie performs such
work at their own risk and responsibility.

## 7. Reporting
Homies and clients are encouraged to report any attempt to arrange Off-platform
engagements to [email]. Reports are handled confidentially. Direct cooperation
through the Platform ensures insurance cover, guarantees, and fair treatment.

## 8. Client notice
Arranging cleaning off-platform with a homie you met through HeyHomie voids the
Company's quality guarantee and insurance for that service and is discouraged. For
your protection, please keep bookings and payments within the app.

## 9. Governing law and severability
This clause is governed by Polish law and is to be interpreted consistently with EU
and Polish competition and labour law. If any provision is found unenforceable, the
remainder stays in effect and the provision is reduced to the maximum enforceable
extent.
