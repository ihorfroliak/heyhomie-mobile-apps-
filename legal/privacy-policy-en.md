## 2. What data we process
- **Account:** first/last name, phone number, email, preferred language.
- **Service:** addresses, access notes, mission details, schedule, ratings and any
  photos you attach to a report.
- **Payments:** handled by our payment provider; we store only non-sensitive
  references (e.g. card brand, last 4 digits), never full card numbers.
- **Technical:** device type, app version, IP, and usage/diagnostic data.

## 4. Recipients / processors
We share data only as needed with processors under data-processing agreements:
SMS (e.g. Twilio), payments (e.g. Stripe), email (e.g. Mailgun), cloud hosting,
and analytics (e.g. Google Analytics). Cleaners ("homies") receive only the data
needed to perform a mission (first name, address, access notes).

## 5. International transfers
Where a processor is outside the EEA, transfers rely on adequacy decisions or

## 6. Retention
We keep data for as long as your account is active and as required by law
(e.g. accounting records for [X] years). After that it is deleted or anonymized.

## 7. Your rights
You have the right to: access, rectification, **erasure** ("right to be
forgotten"), restriction, **data portability**, objection, and to withdraw consent
at any time. You can export your data or delete your account from the app
(Profile → Privacy & data). You may also lodge a complaint with the Polish
supervisory authority — **Prezes Urzędu Ochrony Danych Osobowych (UODO)**,
ul. Stawki 2, 00-193 Warszawa.
